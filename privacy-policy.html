Privacy Policy

TABWISE

Last updated: March 15, 2026 — Effective immediately upon installation

Short version: TABWISE stores everything locally on your device. We do not collect, sell, or transmit your browsing data to any server.

1. What TABWISE Is

TABWISE is a Chrome browser extension that helps you organize, suspend, restore, and manage open browser tabs. It runs locally in your browser and does not require an account for core tab management features.

2. Data We Collect

TABWISE collects no personally identifiable information and does not transmit any data to external servers for core functionality.

The following data is stored locally on your device only using chrome.storage.local:

  • Tab metadata (title, URL, domain, favicon) for active and suspended tabs
  • Your Reading Queue (URLs saved for later)
  • Recently closed sleeping tabs (for recovery, stored temporarily)
  • Your tab budget preference (weekly tab limit)
  • Accessibility settings (e.g., High Contrast mode)
  • Local weekly activity report data (tab counts, sessions)

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your browsing history
  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels
  • We do not share any data with third parties for advertising
  • We do not transmit tab data to any server

4. Permissions Used

TABWISE requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • tabs — Required to read, group, suspend, restore, and manage your open tabs. This data is used only within the extension and never transmitted externally.
  • storage — Required to save your settings, tab registry, reading queue, and report data locally on your device.
  • alarms — Required to trigger the local weekly activity report on a scheduled basis. No network requests are made.

5. Pro / Payment Features

TABWISE Pro is an optional paid upgrade powered by ExtensionPay and Stripe. If you choose to upgrade:

  • Payment is processed by Stripe, which has its own Privacy Policy.
  • ExtensionPay may store your email address to verify payment status.
  • TABWISE receives only a paid/unpaid status flag — no payment card details are ever accessible to the extension.

Payment features are entirely optional. All core tab-management features are available without payment or an account.

6. No Data Sharing or Sale

TABWISE does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your data with any third party. All locally stored data remains entirely under your control and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing extension storage.

7. Children's Privacy

TABWISE does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The extension does not contain any features directed at children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes materially, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be updated. Continued use of TABWISE after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or TABWISE's data practices, you can reach us via the Chrome Web Store support link on the TABWISE extension page.
